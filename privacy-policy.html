Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 06 December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Kids Learning mobile application (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you or your child uses our app.

1. Information We Collect

Kids Learning is designed for children and aims to collect minimal information. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or precise locations.

a) Information Collected Automatically

When you use the app, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version and usage data
  • Crash reports and performance data

This information is used only to improve the app’s performance and user experience. It does not directly identify you or your child.

b) Information Collected Through Third-Party Services (e.g., AdMob)

If the app displays ads using third-party services such as Google AdMob, these services may collect certain information, including:

  • Anonymous identifiers related to your device
  • Ad interaction data
  • General usage information

For child-directed apps, ads are typically shown as non-personalized to comply with children’s privacy regulations.

2. Children’s Privacy

Our app is intended for use by children under the supervision of parents or guardians. We are committed to protecting children’s privacy and complying with applicable laws and regulations.

We do not:

  • Request or collect personally identifiable information from children
  • Require account creation or login
  • Collect precise location data
  • Sell or share personal information with third parties

If you believe that your child may have provided personal information through the app, please contact us using the details below, and we will promptly delete such information.

3. How We Use Information

Any information collected (typically non-personal and aggregated) is used for:

  • Improving app stability and performance
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Enhancing features and user experience
  • Displaying appropriate and child-safe advertisements (if enabled)

We do not use this information to personally identify users.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may include third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob – for showing ads
  • Analytics services – for understanding app usage (if integrated)

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Google AdMob & Google Mobile Ads Policies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information processed by the app from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any non-personal data collected through third-party services is retained in accordance with their respective policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. We recommend that you review this page periodically for any changes.
